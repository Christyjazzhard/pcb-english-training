🎭 情景对话 1：办理值机
员工

早上好！请出示您的护照和订票确认单。
您
Sure, here you go. I'm on the flight to Munich.
好的，给您。我乘坐飞往慕尼黑的航班。
员工
How many bags would you like to check in?

您
Just one. This carry-on I'll take with me.
只有一件。这个随身行李我自己带。
员工
Here is your boarding pass. Your flight departs from Gate B12 at 14:35. Boarding starts at 14:05.

Thank you. Is there a business lounge I can use?
谢谢。请问有商务休息室可以使用吗？

Yes, the business lounge is on Level 3. Have a pleasant flight!
有的，商务休息室在3层。祝您旅途愉快！

💡 德国餐厅礼仪

  • 德国小费通常为消费额的5-10%，现金最佳

  • 商务宴请可主动说：This is on me.（我来买单）

🎭 对话 2：技术参数讨论
客户
Let's discuss the impedance requirements. We need 50-ohm single-ended and 100-ohm differential pairs on a 6-layer board.
来谈谈阻抗要求。我们需要6层板上有50欧单端阻抗和100欧差分阻抗。
您
That's very achievable. We use Polar Software for impedance verification. Our standard tolerance is plus or minus 5%, and we can go to plus or minus 3% if needed.
完全可以实现。我们用Polar软件进行阻抗验证，标准公差±5%，如有需要可达±3%。
客户
What stack-up do you recommend for these requirements?
您推荐什么样的叠层结构来满足这些要求？
您
For a 6-layer board with those specs, I'd recommend FR4 at 4.4 dielectric constant. The trace width for 50-ohm would be approximately 0.127mm. I can prepare a detailed stack-up proposal.
对于这些规格的6层板，我推荐介电常数4.4的FR4。50欧的线宽约为0.127mm，我可以准备详细的叠层设计方案。
客户
What about via design? We're considering blind vias to reduce parasitic capacitance.
过孔设计方面呢？我们在考虑用盲孔来降低寄生电容。
您
Excellent choice. We fully support blind and buried vias. For HDI designs, we offer laser-drilled microvias with a minimum diameter of 0.1mm. This can significantly reduce your board size.
很好的选择。我们完全支持盲孔和埋孔。HDI设计方面，我们提供最小直径0.1mm的激光钻微孔，可显著减小板尺寸。

Our boards need to operate at up to 150 degrees Celsius continuously. Is standard FR4 sufficient?
我们的板需要在150摄氏度环境下持续工作，标准FR4够用吗？

For that temperature range, I'd recommend High Tg FR4 with a glass transition temperature of 170°C. It provides much better thermal stability and is only slightly more expensive.
对于这个温度范围，我推荐玻璃化温度170°C的高Tg FR4，热稳定性好得多，价格仅略高一些。

🎭 对话 4：技术问题与解决方案
客户
We're having signal integrity issues on our current boards. The high-speed signals show significant degradation above 3 GHz.
我们当前的板存在信号完整性问题，高速信号在3GHz以上有明显衰减。
您
I understand. Signal integrity issues at high frequency can be caused by several factors — impedance mismatch, via stubs, or insufficient ground plane coverage. Which PCB manufacturer are you using currently?
我明白。高频信号完整性问题可能由多种因素引起——阻抗不匹配、过孔残桩或接地层覆盖不足。您目前用的是哪家PCB制造商？
客户
We suspect the via design is the main issue. The through-hole vias might be causing reflections.
我们怀疑主要问题在过孔设计，通孔可能导致了反射。
您
That's a very valid concern. Long via stubs cause reflections at high frequencies. The best solution is back-drilling — we remove the stub portion of the via, which dramatically improves signal quality above 3 GHz.
这个判断很正确。过孔残桩在高频下会引起反射。最好的解决方案是背钻——去除过孔的残桩部分，可显著改善3GHz以上的信号质量。
客户
Can you do back-drilling?
你们能做背钻吗？
您
Yes, we offer controlled depth back-drilling. It's one of our core capabilities for high-speed applications. I'd also recommend we do a complimentary DFM review of your Gerber files to check for any other potential issues.
可以，我们提供受控深度背钻，这是我们高速应用的核心能力之一。我也建议免费为您的Gerber文件做DFM分析，检查是否还有其他潜在问题。
客户
A complimentary DFM review sounds great. What do you need from us?
免费DFM分析听起来很好，你们需要我们提供什么？
您
Just send us your Gerber files, drill file, impedance requirements, and stack-up information. We'll review it within 48 hours and send back a detailed DFM report at no charge.
发给我们Gerber文件、钻孔文件、阻抗要求和叠层信息即可，我们48小时内回复详细的DFM报告，完全免费。
客户
Excellent! I'll send the files right after the show.
太好了！展会结束后我马上发文件。
